My main interests are at the intersection of Optics, Computer Vision, and Astronomy. How can we embed inductive biasses from physics into computer vision problems? Similarly, how can we use techniques from computer vision to solve problems in astronomical imaging? I also like to dabble with statistical and deep learning methods, working at the forefront of equivariant machine learning, differentiable programming, and the Julia programming langauge.
